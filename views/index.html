FAQ

I have never been to hackathon, is that OKAY?

Yes, GoodHacks welcomes participants of all skill levels, backgrounds and walks of life. Hackathons are a great way to learn, and we will have mentors available on-site to help you figure out how to make your wildest ideas a reality.

I'm not a Stanford student, can I still participate

Yes, Goodhacks is open to the general public and anyone who is interested in ahcking on cool stuff, promoting social good and solving some of the toughest problems facing non-profits organizations.

How will you choose between applicants

Applicants will be selected based on their interest in GoodHacks, and their past work (side projects, cool schoolwork, involvement with non-profits, etc.)
